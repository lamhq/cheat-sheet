# Work Place

## Join a team

Say hi and introduce yourself, then express your excitement to work with them:
- Hello everyone, i’m Ted. I’m a software engineer. I’m so happy to be a part of this team and i’m looking forward to work with you guys.


## Start a conversation

To start a conversation with a colleague:
- Hi Ted. How’s it going?
- Hey Jim. How are things going?
- Hello Tom. How are you doing?
- Hi Morgan, is it a good time to talk?

To respond:
- Good. Thanks for asking.
- Not bad. How about you?
- Hi, I'm good, thanks. And you?


## Inform news

To acknowledge you're clear about the information shared:
- That's great to hear!
- Thanks for the info. If I have any questions or concerns, I’ll be sure to let you know. Thanks again!
- Alright. Thanks a lot for clarifying that. That’s all I need.
- Yeah, that all makes sense.
- Oh, that’s nice.
- That's great.

To say that you're not clear:
- I'm not sure I understand. Could you please explain that again?
- Could you say that again?

To confirm the information shared:
- If I understood it correctly, ...
