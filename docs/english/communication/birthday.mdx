# Birthday

Ask other person about their birthday:
- When's your birthday?

To say when your birthday is:
- My birthday is on [Ordinal-number] of [Month]. For example: My birthday is on 2nd of June.
- My birthday is on [Month] [Day]. For example: My birthday is on June 2nd.

All are accepted:
- 2nd of June
- the 2nd of June
- June the 2nd
- June 2nd

Use `on` when you say the date and month together, for example: "on June 2nd".

Use `in` when you say the month only, for example: "in June".
