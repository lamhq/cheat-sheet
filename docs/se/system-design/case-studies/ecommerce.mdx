# E-Commerce Platform

## Overview

Architect a highly scalable eCommerce marketplace platform where:
- Merchants can upload and sell their products
- Users can browse, search and buy those products


## Functional Requirements

### General

There're two actors in the system: Merchants and Buyers.

We're selling physical products with a limited inventory. Each product contains:
- Title
- Description
- Categories
- Images
- Optional attributes

### Merchants

**Product management** features:
- Signup
- Create new products
- Update the product properties
- Update product inventory
- View product data

**Product analytics** features. In merchant dashboard, they can: 
- view the current number of visitors to their products page in real time
- see the historical and projected trends about their products performance

### Example Questions

Example questions to collect requirements for Merchants:
- What type of products are we selling? Physical or digital?
- What product information does a merchant provide?
- What data do we need to provide the merchant?
- What operations can a merchant perform?

Example questions to collect requirements for Buyers:
- Can anyone browse/purchase or registration is required?
- Are Product Reviews/Ratings in scope?
- Search capabilities?
- Do we need to design checkout/payment/delivery?
- What Ul do we offer? Browser/Mobile?
