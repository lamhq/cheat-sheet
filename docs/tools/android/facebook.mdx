# Facebook

## Version

This guide is for Facebook Android app version `470.0.0.61.82`


## View app version

Go to `Settings & privacy`, scroll to the bottom setting `About`.


## Turn of Video Autoplay

Go to `Settings & privacy`, under `Preferences`, select `Media`:
- Check `Never Autoplay Videos`.
- Turn off `Videos start with sound`


## Open links in browser

Go to `Settings & privacy`, under `Preferences`, select `Media`:
- Check `Open links in external browser`


## Export Your Data from Facebook

To download your data (including your messages), follow these steps:

1. Click the gear icon to open **Settings & Privacy**.
2. Select **Accounts Center** (the first section in the Settings & Privacy screen).
3. Scroll down to the **Account Settings** section and choose **Your Information and Permissions**.
4. Click **Export Your Information**.

> [!INFO]
> If you're using Facebook on the web, you can go directly to the export feature via this [link](https://accountscenter.facebook.com/info_and_permissions/dyi).
