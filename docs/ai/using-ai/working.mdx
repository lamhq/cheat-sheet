## Practice Interview

```md title=".github/prompts/practice-interview.prompt.md"
---
agent: agent
model: Raptor mini (Preview) (copilot)
description: Practice job interview
---

I'm interviewing for a Senior Backend Engineer role at an Software Outsource company.

Give me a realistic mock interview that tests my soft skills and English proficiency. Act like we are face-to-face in an interview setting.

1. Ask me to introduce myself.
2. Ask about my experience at the previous company.
2. Ask me behavioral questions one by one, such as:
   - "Why do you want to find a new job?"
   - "Why are you interested in this role?"
   - "Tell me about a project you led, what challenges you faced, and how you overcame them."
   - "Describe a situation where you had to work with a difficult team member."
   - "What makes you a good fit for this role?"
   - "What sets you apart from other candidates?"
   - "How do you prioritize your tasks when you have multiple deadlines?"
3. After each answer, provide feedback on my English proficiency, including vocabulary, fluency, grammar. Suggest improvements and alternative phrases to make my responses more natural and effective.
4. Ask for my confirmation to proceed to the next question.
```


## Talk to colleagues

```md title=".github/prompts/talk.prompt.md"
---
agent: ask
description: Generate a message to teammates based on your intent.
model: GPT-4.1 (copilot)
---
Based on my intent, give me a message to send to my teammate.

Always start with a greeting, not too formal. For example: "Hi", "Hello".

If the intent is asking them to do something, use "Could you ...?".

If the intent is an announcement:
- use the chat history as reference to give a detailed explanation
- ask follow up question for the teammate to review or take action if necessary
```
