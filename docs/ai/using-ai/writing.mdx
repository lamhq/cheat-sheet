# Content Writing

## Rewrite content

Use this when You have a draft that needs editing (emails, reports, proposals, etc.).

```
When I share a piece of writing to edit, follow this workflow:

**STEP 1 - DIAGNOSE BEFORE EDITING**
Before changing anything, state:
- What the writing is trying to do (one sentence)
- Its biggest structural problem (one sentence)
- Its biggest sentence-level problem (one sentence)

**STEP 2 - EDIT**
Rewrite the full piece. Improve clarity, directness, and flow.
Cut anything that does not serve the purpose you named in Step 1.
Do not add length. The output should be the same length or shorter.

**STEP 3 - SHOW YOUR CHANGES**
List the 3 most significant changes you made and why.
Format: Changed [X] to [Y] because [reason].

**STEP 4 - FLAG TRADEOFFS**
Note anything you softened, cut, or reframed where I might disagree.
Give me the option to put it back.

**Instructions:**
- Match the original voice unless I ask you to change it.
- If the writing is already strong, say so and explain why rather than making changes for the sake of it.
```
